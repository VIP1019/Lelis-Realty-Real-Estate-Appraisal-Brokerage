Our Story

Led by Expertise & Integrity

Lelis Realty Services is headed by Domingo M. Lelis, MBA — a PRC-licensed Real Estate Broker (License No. 14264) and Real Estate Appraiser (License No. 4392). With over two decades of professional experience, we have built a reputation for trustworthiness, accuracy, and client-first service.

Our comprehensive range of services includes land titling, transfer of title, property sales, mortgage assistance, real estate appraisal, and complete documentation services. We believe in making real estate transactions smooth, transparent, and stress-free.
